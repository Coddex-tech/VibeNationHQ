DMCA Policy

VibeNation respects the intellectual property rights of others and is committed to complying with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If you believe that your copyrighted material has been posted or shared on our website without authorization, please follow the procedures below to submit a DMCA takedown notice.

How to File a DMCA Complaint

Your notice must include the following information to be processed:

  • Your full legal name and accurate contact information (email and phone).
  • A clear description or link to the copyrighted work that you claim has been infringed.
  • A statement affirming that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on behalf of the owner.
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

Send your DMCA notice to: support@vibenation.com

Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed or disabled by mistake, you may submit a counter-notice to the same email address above. Once verified, we may restore the content in accordance with DMCA procedures. All counter-notifications must comply with legal requirements under the DMCA.
